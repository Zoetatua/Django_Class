Dragon Training

To train your dragon, start by building trust and forming a bond. Learn about your dragon’s unique abilities and temperament. Use positive reinforcement, patience, and consistent routines to teach commands and tricks. Provide a safe environment, healthy food, and regular exercise. Remember, every dragon is different—adapt your training methods to suit your dragon’s personality for the best results!
